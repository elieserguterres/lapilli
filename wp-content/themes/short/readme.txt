== Description ==

Short WordPress theme ideal for a blog, personal portfolio, business website, WooCommerce storefront, shop, business agencies, finance, corporate, travel, law firms, agency, photography, recipes, design, portfolio, arts, blogs, education, university portal, consulting, church, restaurant, medical and so on, it has a multipurpose design, widgetized footer, blog/news page and a clean look. Short is responsive, SEO friendly, WPML, Polylang Translation WordPress Plugin, Woocommerce, Contact Form 7, translation and RTL ready, Short is the perfect theme for you! check live preview: https://demo.themeansar.com/short/lite/
